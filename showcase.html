Business Showcase — Selected Projects

Here are three projects I’ve worked on to strengthen my skills in web development, UX, and collaborative design. Each project reflects real-world challenges, my approach, and the results I achieved.

Personal Portfolio Website (Independent Project, 2024)

My Role: Designer & Developer

The Problem: I wanted to create a personal portfolio to showcase my skills and projects, balancing design aesthetics with clarity and accessibility.

What I Did: I designed the layout in Figma, built it with HTML and CSS, and focused on making it fully responsive and user-friendly. I also added subtle animations for engagement.

The Outcome: The portfolio became a polished site I could use for job applications, improving my front-end development skills and confidence in independent work.

Accessibility Website Audit (Independent Project, 2024)

My Role: Researcher & Implementer

The Problem: Many small websites lacked accessibility features, making navigation difficult for users with screen readers or visual impairments.

What I Did: I performed a full accessibility audit on a local business website, identified issues like low contrast and missing alt text, and suggested improvements. I created a simple prototype demonstrating better accessibility practices.

The Outcome: The prototype addressed key accessibility issues and provided clear recommendations for future development, giving me practical experience in inclusive design.

Group Presentation: Business Process Analysis (Group Assignment, 2024)

My Role: Researcher & Slide Designer

The Problem: Our group had to analyze a real business process and present findings in a clear, engaging way. Coordinating work and aligning everyone’s ideas was challenging.

What I Did: I gathered data, designed slides for clarity and impact, and collaborated to integrate everyone’s research. I also helped manage deadlines and ensure the presentation was cohesive.

The Outcome: The presentation was well-received, showing a clear understanding of the process. I learned valuable teamwork skills, communication, and how to compromise when working in a group.
