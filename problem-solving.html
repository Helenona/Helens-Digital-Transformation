Restaurant Digital Transformation — Problem-Solving

Business context

As CTO for a local restaurant, the goal is to modernise customer engagement: enable simple online bookings, capture feedback, and increase social reach. The prototype below demonstrates a lightweight, low-friction approach that can scale into a full booking system later.

Design rationale

      
Key choices:

      
    
        
  • Linear flow: explanation → action (form) → proof (contacts & socials) reduces uncertainty and supports conversion.
    • 
        
  • Lightweight form: required fields only to reduce abandonment; client validation ensures data quality without back-end complexity.
    • 
        
  • Email generation: using mailto: creates a simple handoff for restaurants to process requests without building a full booking system immediately.
    • 
        
  • Social links: provide social proof and a channel for marketing; adding structured contact details increases trust.
